Moto Baku Academy – Newly Opened Central Branch (Water Sports Palace)

Moto Baku Academy is constantly evolving and creating new opportunities to get closer to motorcycle enthusiasts. Our new branch, located in the city center – Water Sports Palace, is now operational.
This branch offers a more comfortable and accessible training environment for both Moto Baku Academy students and professional riders.

What We Offer

  • Safe and effective motorcycle lessons with professional instructors
  • Both outdoor and indoor training areas
  • Theoretical and practical lessons tailored for Moto Baku Academy
  • Advanced training and stunt lessons for professionals
  • Workshops on safety and proper use of equipment

Advantages of the New Branch

  • Accessible location in the city center
  • Modern infrastructure and training equipment
  • Capacity to accept more students

Our Goal

At Moto Baku Academy, our goal is to instill in every student not only how to ride a motorcycle but also a culture of safe riding. With our new branch at the Water Sports Palace, we can bring this mission to a wider audience.

👉 Join the Moto Baku Academy family and learn motorcycle riding with a professional approach!
